 FARDU WAJIB

* Wallah ta’abna wa ta’abraha Ma ‘akum yaa habaayib
Wamahma fil hawa dubna Alaina fardu wajib
Alaina nakramiszin lahuttu was tidal a’in
Waman ya’ syaqidhahi watahla lil matayib
*
Bidarbil hubbi yaa ma u hibbi dzagatil wil
Wamin gasdi min adabi agamu wasli aukil
Wanaa khilli mahalla dalaa tibati dalla
Li anna fil hawa adam saghiri sinni jahil
*

# Fii dab’il ‘aini wallah rahwainiza rafullah
Waa min hama khanaina dziwa’adz waa adalla
Dzahirna hillayali mughinni mur rihalii
Wadina warridha filhub wajib fardu wajib
